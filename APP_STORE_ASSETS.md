## App Description

### Short Description (Promotional Text - 170 characters)
Connect with fellow surfers, check real-time surf conditions, plan epic trips together, and buy/sell gear. Your all-in-one surf community app.

### Full Description (4000 characters max)

Board Meeting is the ultimate social app for surfers who want to connect with like-minded wave riders, check surf conditions, and plan unforgettable surf trips together.

**FIND YOUR SURF BUDDY**
Swipe through profiles of local surfers and find your perfect surf partner. Whether you're a beginner looking for someone to learn with or an experienced rider seeking new adventures, Board Meeting connects you with surfers who match your vibe and skill level.

**REAL-TIME SURF REPORTS**
Check accurate surf forecasts for 600+ spots worldwide, including:
- California (Mavericks, Trestles, Huntington Beach, and more)
- Hawaii (Pipeline, Waimea, North Shore spots)
- East Coast USA (New Jersey, Florida, Outer Banks)
- International destinations (Indonesia, Australia, Europe)

Get wave height, swell direction, wind conditions, and surf ratings. Premium members unlock full 7-day forecasts.

**PLAN SURF TRIPS**
Organize group surf trips with ease. Find travel buddies to split costs, discover new destinations, and create shared itineraries. Save money and travel safely with fellow surfers.

**SURF GEAR MARKETPLACE**
Buy, sell, and trade surfboards, wetsuits, and gear with verified community members. Location-based search helps you find deals nearby.

**SOCIAL FEED**
Share your sessions, post photos, celebrate achievements, and stay connected with the surfing community.

**PROFILE & STATS**
Track your surfing stats including top speed, biggest wave, and longest ride. Show off tricks you've mastered and build your surfer profile.

Download Board Meeting today and join thousands of surfers connecting, riding, and exploring together!
